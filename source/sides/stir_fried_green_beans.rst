Stir fried green beans
----------------------

:term:`Easy`, :term:`One pot`, :term:`Vegetarian`

Source: Jingwen via :cite:cts:`xiachufang:fried_beans`.

.. image:: /graphics/stir_fried_green_beans.jpg
   :width: 40%
   :align: center

Ingredients
^^^^^^^^^^^

* :term:`Green beans`
* 2 or 3 cloves of :term:`Garlic`

And for seasoning:

* :term:`Dry chilli` flakes (decent amount) and chilli powder (a little)
* :term:`Sichuan pepper`
* :term:`Soy sauce`
* :term:`MSG`

Steps
^^^^^

Cut the beans into bite sized pieces.
Stir fry them for a few minutes.

Set aside (or use a different pan), gently fry the sliced garlic with the pepper and chilli flakes until fragrant.
Add back the beans, chilli powder  and stir fry some more.

Add a tablespoon of soy sauce, some MSG, and stir to combine.
