Egg fried rice *v2*
-------------------

:term:`Medium`

An actual authentic version.
Source: Jingwen via :cite:cts:`xiachufang:fried_rice`.

Ingredients
^^^^^^^^^^^

* Leftover :term:`Rice`
* :term:`Eggs <Egg>`, about 1 per 100g of rice
* A small sausage or some leftover meat
* :term:`Green onion`
* 1 tablespoon of pickled :term:`chilli pepper` 

Some vegetables, most work as long as they don't have too much moisture. Some good choices are

* :term:`Mushroom`
* :term:`Cucumber`
* :term:`Peas`
* :term:`Carrot`
* (cooked) :term:`Corn`
* :term:`Bell pepper`

Steps
^^^^^

Separate the egg white and yolk.

Fry the whites into a pancake and split it into medium chunks.
You can do it with your spatula on the frying pan, but the goal is to get bigger pieces than regular scrambled eggs.
Set them aside.

Dice all the vegetables into small pieces, including the white part of the green onion, and stir fry with some oil on high heat, together with the meat if necessary.
Set them aside.
Naturally, some vegetables like cucumber don't require this step.

Thoroughly mix the egg yolks into the rice with a spoon.
Fry it on medium heat with more oil, until the rice is completely dry.

Add back all the ingredients, including the chilli peppers.
Combine everything and adjust the salt.
Serve with green onion on top.
