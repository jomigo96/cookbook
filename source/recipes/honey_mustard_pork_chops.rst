Honey mustard pork chops
------------------------

:term:`Easy`

.. image:: /graphics/honey_mustard_pork_chops.jpg
   :width: 35%
   :align: center

Ingredients
^^^^^^^^^^^

For two:

* :term:`Pork` chops, or a loin steak cut. Rather thick cuts are best.
* 2 tablespoons :term:`Mustard`
* 1 tablespoon :term:`Honey`
* ½ tablespoon :term:`Soy sauce` or :term:`Worcestershire sauce`
* If the mustard is not very strong, a splash of :term:`Vinegar`

Steps
^^^^^

Mix all sauces with a bit of ground pepper.

Preheat a pan with some oil.
When very hot, cook the pork for 2 or 3 minutes on each side, letting it make a brown crust.

Lower the heat and add the sauce.
Keep flipping the meat and stirring it around, letting it caramelize for around 5 minutes.
