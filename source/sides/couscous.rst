.. _couscous:

Couscous
--------

Ingredients
^^^^^^^^^^^

* :term:`Couscous`

Steps
^^^^^

Boil a kettle.
In a pot, put 1 part boiling water, a bit of salt, and a splash of olive oil.
Do not place the pot on the stove.
Put 1 part couscous and cover.

It's ready in 5 minutes, fluff it with a fork.
